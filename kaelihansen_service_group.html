About This Work

Some of us grow best in circle

There is something irreplaceable about being witnessed by others on the same journey. Group Journeys offer that — a held, intentional space where your creative expression is shared, received, and honored.

I offer weekend workshops (both online and in-person) and two-month online courses in Creative Expression. Each course includes a journal with creative expression prompts, weekly Zoom meetings to share your experiences and creative work with the group, and ongoing support in getting to your core in a safe, community-held space.

These are not performance spaces. They are spaces of exploration, vulnerability, and mutual encouragement. Every voice belongs. Every expression has value.

You may be…

  • Someone who thrives in community and shared experience
  • Looking for accountability and gentle encouragement
  • Curious about creative expression but want to explore with others
  • Feeling isolated in your caregiving or spiritual journey
  • Ready to be witnessed and to witness others
  • Interested in structured, supported creative practice

What’s included
