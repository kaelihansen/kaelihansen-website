About This Work

Reflect, expand, and go deeper

As a healing facilitator and spiritual aspirant, I accompany you on your journey by helping you find the deeper questions that need to be asked. As your partner in this work, I am the observer — and you are the expert in your own life.

I will walk beside you while you unfold your own answers on your seeker’s journey, providing a sounding board and a safe, spacious container for your unfolding. This work is rooted in decades of meditation practice, graduate study in consciousness and transpersonal psychology, and a deep commitment to walking the path myself.

We engage in deeper conversations about consciousness, purpose, and inner life. Meaningful inner shifts emerge not from being told what to think, but from being held in the kind of presence that allows truth to surface naturally.

You may be…

  • Questioning your direction in life
  • Looking for something deeper or more meaningful
  • Interested in deepening your spirituality
  • Seeking healing on a spiritual level
  • Trying to find meaning and purpose
  • Ready for a partner who listens without judgment
