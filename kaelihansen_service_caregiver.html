About This Work

Find steady footing in the midst of responsibility

Caregiving is one of the most demanding, most invisible, and most meaningful things a person can do. Whether you are caring for an aging parent, a partner with chronic illness, or a child with complex needs — the weight of it is real, and it deserves real support.

Together, we work with emotional overwhelm, burnout, and the quiet loss of self that caregiving can bring. Through exercises to center yourself, listen to your body, and reconnect with your core needs, we build a practice of self-care that can realistically fit into your life.

We will also explore small, sustainable actions you can take daily, weekly, and monthly — not grand gestures, but honest, grounded care for the person who keeps everything going.

You may be…

  • Emotionally exhausted or burned out
  • Disconnected from your own identity
  • Carrying more than you can process alone
  • Longing for creative expression but unsure where to begin
  • Navigating grief alongside caregiving
  • Searching for meaning amid the demands of daily care

What we work on together

01
Grounding & Centering
Exercises to help you come back to yourself — even in the midst of the most demanding seasons.
02
Creative Self-Care
Using creative expression as a tool for processing, releasing, and returning to who you are beneath the caregiver role.
03
Sustainable Action
Small, realistic self-care practices you can weave into your actual life — not an idealized version of it.
